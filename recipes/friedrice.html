FRIED RICE

fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped green beans
  • 1/2 cup chopped spring onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped green chillies

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a wok or a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the onions, bell peppers, carrots, green beans, spring onions, celery, cabbage, mushrooms, garlic, ginger, and green chillies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  2. Add the cooked rice and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until the rice is heated through. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
