CHICKEN NOODLES

Chicken Noodles is a delicious and easy recipe that is perfect for lunch or dinner. It is a great way to use up leftover chicken and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 pound fresh egg noodles
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, chicken broth, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  3. Add garlic and ginger to the skillet and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add carrots, bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Return chicken to skillet and add cooked noodles and sauce. Toss to combine and cook until heated through, about 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
