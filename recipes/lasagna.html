LASAGNA

DESCRIPTION

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

INGREDIENTS

  • Beef: This easy lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use ground turkey for a lighter option.
  • Onion and Garlic: These aromatics add a ton of flavor to the meat sauce.
  • Tomato Sauce: Use your favorite jarred marinara sauce or homemade tomato sauce.
  • Tomato Paste: This adds a rich, concentrated tomato flavor to the sauce.
  • Seasonings: A blend of dried basil, oregano, and crushed red pepper flakes add a ton of flavor to the sauce.
  • Lasagna Noodles: No-boil lasagna noodles make this recipe a breeze. You can also use regular lasagna noodles, but you’ll need to cook them first.
  • Ricotta Cheese: This creamy cheese adds a ton of flavor and richness to the lasagna.
  • Egg: The egg helps bind the ricotta cheese together.
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese: This cheese adds a ton of gooey, melty goodness to the lasagna.
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese: This cheese adds a nutty, salty flavor to the lasagna.
  • Fresh Parsley: This herb adds a pop of color and freshness to the lasagna.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until no pink remains. Drain the fat.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes to the skillet. Stir to combine. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 cup of the mozzarella cheese, and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese. Stir to combine.
  4. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9×13-inch baking dish. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, and 1 cup of the meat sauce. Repeat the layers twice more.
  5. Top with the remaining 3 lasagna noodles and the remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
